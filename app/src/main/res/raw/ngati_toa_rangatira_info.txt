This rohe map represents the contemporary area over which Ngāti Toa Rangatira exercises kaitiakitanga for the purposes of the Resource Management Act 1991. The historical Area of Interest agreed between Ngāti Toa Rangatira and the Crown in the Deed of Settlement signed on 7 December 2012 is more extensive.
