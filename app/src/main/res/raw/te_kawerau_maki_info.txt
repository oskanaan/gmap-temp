This rohe map represents the area over which Te Kawerau a Maki exercises kaitiakitanga for the purposes of the Resource Management Act 1991 and is based on the Area of Interest agreed between Te Kawerau a Maki and the Crown in the Deed of Settlement signed on 22 February 2014.
