This rohe map represents the area over which Ngāti Tūwharetoa (Bay of Plenty) exercises kaitiakitanga for the purposes of the Resource Management Act 1991 and is based on the Area of Interest agreed between Ngāti Tūwharetoa (Bay of Plenty) and the Crown in the Deed of Settlement signed on 6 June 2003.
