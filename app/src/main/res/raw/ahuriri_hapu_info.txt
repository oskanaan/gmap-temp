This rohe map represents the area over which Ahuriri Hapū exercises kaitiakitanga for the purposes of the Resource Management Act 1991 and is based on the Area of Interest agreed between Ahuriri Hapū and the Crown in the Deed of Settlement signed on 02 November 2016.
