This rohe map represents the area over which Ngāti Kurī exercises kaitiakitanga for the purposes of the Resource Management Act 1991 and is based on the Area of Interest agreed between Ngāti Kurī and the Crown in the Deed of Settlement signed on 7 February 2014. Ngāti Kurī Area of Interest from the Deed of Settlement [JPG, 104KB].

The rohe of Ngāti Kurī includes the Kermadec and Three Kings Islands.


This rohe extends into the regions or districts of these local authorities:
Regional Council
Northland Regional Council
Territorial Authority
Far North District Council
